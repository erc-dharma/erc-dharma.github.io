About Us

The DHARMA project ERC n°809994 has been launched on May 1st 2019. It is a 6-year project (2019-2025) financed on an ERC Synergy Grant 2018 through the European Union’s Research and Innovation Programme Horizon 2020.

The project’s three Principal Investigators are Emmanuel Francis (CEIAS), Arlo Griffiths (EFEO) and Annette Schmiedchen (UBER), while Florinda De Simini will lead project activities at a fourth beneficiary, the University « L’Orientale » of Naples.
